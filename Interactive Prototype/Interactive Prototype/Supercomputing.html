High Performance Computing and Supercomputing
  • Center for Research Computing Resources: Pitt Center for Research Computing provides different types of hardware for different advanced computing needs.
  • Supercomputing Allocation with PSC: A primary mission of the Pittsburgh Supercomputing Center is to train students, including undergraduates, in high performance computing. To this end, PSC offers Coursework allocations which are grants of free supercomputing time to supplement other teaching tools.
